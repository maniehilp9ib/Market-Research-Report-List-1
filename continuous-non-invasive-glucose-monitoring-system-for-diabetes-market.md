<p><p>The Continuous Non-invasive Glucose Monitoring System (CNGMS) for diabetes includes two primary market types: wearable CNGMS and implantable CNGMS. Wearable CNGMS utilizes external devices, such as patches or wristbands, that monitor glucose levels through the skin without the need for needles. In contrast, implantable CNGMS involves devices surgically placed under the skin that provide continuous glucose readings. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management by offering real-time data, improving patient convenience, and reducing the pain associated with traditional monitoring methods.</p></p>
