<p><p>The Metal Cutting Machine (MCM) market includes various technologies for precise metal fabrication. Laser cutting machines utilize focused beams of light for intricate designs with minimal heat-affected zones. Waterjet cutting machines employ high-pressure water mixed with abrasives, ideal for cutting hard materials without thermal distortion. Plasma cutting machines use ionized gas to cut through metals quickly, while flame cutting machines utilize a high-temperature flame for thicker materials. Other methods may include mechanical or electrical cutting techniques, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
