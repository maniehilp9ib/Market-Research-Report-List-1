<p><p>Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) systems are categorized into three main types: Classical TLC, High-Performance TLC (HPTLC), and Preparative TLC (PLC). Classical TLC involves a simple setup with basic glass plates and is often used for routine analysis. HPTLC employs advanced techniques for enhanced resolution and sensitivity, allowing for the simultaneous analysis of multiple samples. Preparative TLC focuses on isolating and purifying compounds from mixtures, making it valuable in research and pharmaceutical applications. Each type serves distinct analytical and preparative needs in various industries.</p></p>
