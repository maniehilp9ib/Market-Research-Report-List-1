<p><strong>The High Voltage Power Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Type Transformer</li><li>Oil-Immersed Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage power transformer market comprises two primary types: dry-type transformers and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling and are often favored in urban areas due to reduced fire risk and maintenance needs. In contrast, oil-immersed transformers use liquid insulation for better heat dissipation, making them suitable for larger power applications and outdoor installations. Each type serves distinct operational environments, balancing factors like safety, efficiency, and cost to meet diverse industrial and utility demands.</p></p>
