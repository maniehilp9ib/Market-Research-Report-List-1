<p><p>The pea protein ingredients market consists of three main types: isolates, concentrates, and other formulations. Isolates contain the highest protein content, typically exceeding 80%, and are refined to remove most carbohydrates and fats, making them ideal for protein-rich products. Concentrates contain around 70% protein, retaining more nutrients and flavor, suitable for a wider range of food applications. Other market types may include textured pea protein or blends, catering to specific dietary needs and preferences in various food products.</p></p>
