<p><p>The circulator bath fluid market is divided into two main types: thermal circulating fluid and cold circulating fluid. Thermal circulating fluids are designed to operate at high temperatures, providing reliable heat transfer for applications like heating and temperature control in laboratories and industrial processes. In contrast, cold circulating fluids are used for cooling applications, efficiently removing heat from processes and maintaining low temperatures, crucial for preserving temperature-sensitive materials and ensuring optimal performance in various scientific and industrial settings.</p></p>
