<p><p>The Damp-Proof LED Linear Luminaire market features various types classified by their Ingress Protection (IP) ratings, indicating their resistance to moisture and dust. IP64 offers dust-tight protection and splash resistance. IP65 provides dust-tightness and protection against low-pressure water jets. IP66 enhances this with protection against powerful water jets. IP67 is waterproof and can be submerged up to 1 meter for a brief duration, while IP68 allows for continuous immersion in water beyond 1 meter, catering to diverse environments and installations.</p></p>
