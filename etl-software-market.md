<p><p>The ETL software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ETL tools offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness by enabling services over the internet, which eliminates the need for significant hardware investments. They facilitate easy integration and access to data from various locations. On-premises ETL software, conversely, is installed locally on company servers, providing enhanced control over data security and compliance but requiring more significant upfront costs and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
