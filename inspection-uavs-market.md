<p><p>Inspection UAVs, or Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, are categorized primarily into three types: Rotary Airfoil, Fixed-Wing, and Others. Rotary Airfoil UAVs, commonly known as drones, offer vertical takeoff and are ideal for precise inspections in confined spaces. Fixed-Wing UAVs provide longer flight durations and greater coverage, making them suitable for expansive areas like pipelines and power lines. The "Others" category encompasses hybrid models that combine features of both types, catering to specialized inspection needs across various industries.</p></p>
