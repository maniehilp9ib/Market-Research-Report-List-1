<p><p>The dual-side aligners market comprises three main types: Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Fully Automatic. Semi-Automatic aligners streamline some functions with mechanized assistance, enhancing efficiency while still requiring human intervention. Fully Automatic aligners utilize advanced technologies to conduct operations autonomously, optimizing precision and reducing human error. Each type caters to varying needs from simplicity and cost-effectiveness to high efficiency and automation.</p></p>
