<p><ul><li>Active Glasses</li><li>Passive Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Glass market for buildings is categorized into active and passive glasses. Active glasses utilize technologies like electrochromic and thermochromic systems, allowing users to control transparency and light transmission dynamically, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort. In contrast, passive glasses have fixed properties, adjusting to environmental changes without external input, often incorporating materials that naturally regulate temperature and glare. Both types contribute to sustainable building practices, improving aesthetic value while reducing energy consumption and enhancing occupant experience.</p></p>
